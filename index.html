Solar Generators & Power Systems

Since 2005, Mobile Solar has been engineering, manufacturing and selling a variety of solar generators, solar light towers, transportable systems built in steel containers, and pre-wired equipment packages.  Our units are silent, reliable, resilient and proudly made in the U.S.A.  Our on-going commitment to quality and continued improvement set our products light years apart from the competition. 
We manufacture these units to order and offer an array of options and custom upgrades.  Our team has decades of experience working with battery-based photovoltaics and a passion for alternative energy.  We have also partnered with Sun for Rent – which offers low-cost short term generator rentals of our MS-Series units and solar light towers.

Our Products

Our design philosophy is simple: “safe, rugged, reliable.”  And we think it shows.  Our solar trailers are easy to tow and set up, while providing a durable, attractive solution to the off-grid power need.
From our smallest model to the industrial strength of our MS-437, all of our generators supply pure sinewave current more consistent than that from your utility.  As a bonus, they operate independent of the grid and rely on massive energy banks to provide power through days of bad weather.
And since our solar trailers are not attached to the ground, they shouldn’t require permits or inspections —nor should they increase your property taxes. We manufacture these units to order.  If there is something you do not see, do not hesitate to ask us for a quote.  
Whether powering a job-site office trailer, running remote LIDAR systems or powering a home during a grid outage, you do not have to settle for a noisy, stinky generator that relies on increasingly expensive energy sources.  We have years of experience working with battery-based photovoltaics and a passion for alternative energy sources.  Contact us now and let us find a mobile solar generator that will suit your needs and budget.
